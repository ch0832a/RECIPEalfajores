RECIPE

ALFAJORES

Soft, delicate cookies originally from South America. Smooth and satiny texture filled with dulce de leche that holds both cookies together.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of powdered sugar
  • 1 cup of corn starch
  • 4 sticks of butter

    • Procedure

    Combine all the ingredients in the mixer, with the kneading hook. Once it has a consistency that looks easy to knead, remove it from the mixer and stretch it with a rolling pin. Cut the dough with a round cookie cutter. Bake at 300 degrees Fahrenheit for about 8 minutes. Cookies should look white and not golden. When cold, fill them with dulce de leche and sprinkle powdered sugar.
